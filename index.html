Help Every Juan in This time of Pandemic

STORY

As every individual like students,teachers and parents are struggling in this time of pandemic because of COVID-19.Juan a Information Technology student a top student in academic excellence in their class but due to circumstances he's family is not privilege enough to afford a own personal computer and internet to properly attend in online learning.I created this website with a purpose to help a student like Juan to cope up in Online Class. Also in this website are some example why students are having a circumstances to achieve academic excellence in this pandemic.

Challenges

Technical Difficulties

Not all of us have a decent computer devices and some devices suddenly shutting down in itself.And the some have a poor internet connection so it's hard to cope up in the online class.

Financial Issues

Some students are not privilege enough to buy gadgets or personal computers to keep up on online class.Also the internet connection here in the philippines is a bit expensive.

Staying Motivated

Given that students may not be attending class at a set time on a physical campus, finding the motivation to get started on coursework can be difficult, experts say.
